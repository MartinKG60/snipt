Remote Work Challenge: Asynchronous Communication

Remote teams lose the advantage of sitting together. Without instant feedback, clarification takes hours or days. Screenshots solve this.

Snipt makes sharing visual information instant and effortless. Someone in New York can send a screenshot to someone in Berlin, and both can reference the same visual context without needing a meeting.
